Welcome to the documentation for the **Toy Store Management App**—a comprehensive, full-stack solution designed to streamline every aspect of your international toy store.

## What this App Does

The Toy Store Management App provides:

- **Secure Authentication**: Log in as an admin via a user-friendly login page.
- **Real-Time Dashboard**: Monitor key metrics including customer counts, weekly order trends, geographic distribution of orders, and breakdown of toy categories.
- **Customer & Order Management**: Perform full CRUD operations on customers, orders, and products through an intuitive management interface.
- **PDF Reporting**: Export detailed order reports in PDF format, customizable to your needs.
